---

**EnvDex** is a robust, beautifully designed application that empowers explorers, academic researchers, and biology enthusiasts to capture, catalog, and search their environmental observations (plants, animals, fungi) without needing an internet connection.

It functions as a premium "Pokedex" for the natural world—designed for use deep in the wilderness where cell service is non-existent.

## 🚀 Key Features

- 🌿 **Offline-First Architecture**: Built entirely on top of Realm Database. Every observation, photo, and setting is saved locally to your device, ensuring 100% functionality even when disconnected from the internet.
- 🔒 **Multi-User Sandboxing**: Share the same physical device among multiple naturalists securely. The "Hash ID" login system ensures that your observations are strictly isolated to your profile.
- 📸 **Custom Field Camera**: A tailored camera experience with Macro/Zoom controls, Flash modes, and precise Grid Overlays specifically designed for wildlife photography.
- 🧠 **Automatic Species Merging**: When you log a new encounter, EnvDex automatically analyzes your catalog. If it's a new species, a brand-new Pokedex card is minted. If it's an existing species, the encounter is elegantly nested into its archive history.
- 🗑️ **Soft Deletion & Recycle Bin**: Never lose data by accident in the field. Deleted observations are moved to a temporary recycle bin for easy restoration.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

EnvDex utilizes a modern, highly optimized mobile stack to deliver fluid, native performance:

- **Framework**: [Expo 56](https://expo.dev/) & React Native 0.85
- **Local Database**: [@realm/react](https://realm.io/) (Object-Oriented, Reactive Data Layer)
- **Styling**: [NativeWind](https://www.nativewind.dev/) (Tailwind CSS for React Native)
- **State Management**: [Zustand](https://github.com/pmndrs/zustand)
- **Navigation**: Expo Router (File-based routing)
- **Media Hardware**: Expo Camera, Expo Media Library, Expo Image Picker
